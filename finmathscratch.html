This scratch game calculates increasing annuity of a one time investment. The financial advisor aka the office elephant asks you how much you are planning to invest and tells you how much money you would have in the future!

TThis game is a retirement savings calculate. You can calculate how much you need to put into your savings account monthly to be able to quit working forever after a certain age. Note: While plugging in the interest rate don't use percent sign (for example type 7 and not 7%). Similiarly while inputing money you plan to have don't use dollar sign or commas( for example if you want to save $100,000, just type 100000).
